Hardcourt

Hardcourt leagues are played at Metro State gym on the Auraria Campus. The leagues consist of women's sixes on Sundays and coed sixes on Mondays, Tuesdays, Wednesdays and Thursdays. Women's is only Division 1, while coed has a Division 1, Division 2 and Division 3 each night. Each team plays three games a night and the games are to 25. There are referees and the time slot for each team is limited to an hour.

Grass

Wash Park is the central location for most of the grass leagues. There are over 8 divisions and there are leagues on every night from Sunday through Friday. Most divisions are coed fours, but there are lower level divisions for coed sixes. Each week, your team will play 6 games. You will play three against the first team that you match up with and another three against the second team that you match up with.

SOB

Our South of the Border Vacations are truly amazing. Ask anyone that has been. We have the best groups, the best all-inclusive hotels and the best locations. You will play a ton of volleyball. There is a doubles tournament that begins on the second day you arrive. This tournament is broken into three Divisions in both the mens and the womens. In addition, there is a divisional coed doubles tournament and a coed fours rotating pairs tournament, as well.
